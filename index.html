VISIT INDIA

Explore India's majestic forts, serene temples, bustling bazaars, and savor its rich culinary delights. Unforgettable adventures await!

Caving

Meghalaya, India's hidden gem, boasts captivating caves, waterfalls, and mountains. Explore unique formations and rare species in accessible caves like Arwah, or seek thrills in unconquered ones.

Dune Bashing

Craving desert adventure? Jaisalmer offers top dune bashing in India. Feel the adrenaline on 13-16ft-high golden sand dunes, perfect for ATV quad bike off-roading. Thrills await!

Skiing

Discover India's hidden winter-sports gem in Kashmir's snow-capped Himalayas. Gulmarg offers unmatched ski slopes, including gondola rides to 13,400 feet and thrilling heli-skiing adventures.
